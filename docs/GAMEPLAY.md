# Ghost Catcher Tycoon - Gameplay Guide

## Game Flow

### Main Loop
1. **Click to Charge** → Increase vacuum power
2. **Catch Ghosts** → In different zones
3. **Return to HQ** → Ghosts generate Ecto-Energy
4. **Train Ghosts** → Improve their stats
5. **Upgrade HQ** → Unlock new rooms and features
6. **Expand** → Unlock new zones
7. **Repeat** → Infinite progression

## Core Mechanics

### Vacuum Charging
- **How it works**: Click the vacuum button to charge power
- **Power bar**: Shows current charge level (0-100%)
- **Catch probability**: Higher charge = higher chance to catch
- **Energy cost**: Costs 0 energy (free to charge)
- **Duration**: Instant per click (no cooldown)

**Example**:
- 25% charge: 30% catch chance
- 50% charge: 60% catch chance
- 100% charge: 95% catch chance

### Ghost Catching
- **Location**: Catch ghosts in different zones
- **Requirement**: Vacuum must be charged
- **Success**: Random based on vacuum charge + ghost difficulty
- **Failure**: Vacuum recharges, ghost respawns later
- **Cooldown**: 2 seconds between attempts

**Ghost Types by Zone**:
- **Forest**: Easy ghosts (Common, Uncommon)
- **Graveyard**: Medium ghosts (Uncommon, Rare)
- **Haunted Mansion**: Hard ghosts (Rare, Legendary)
- **Boss Zone**: Boss ghosts (Legendary+)

### Ghost Rarities

| Rarity | Color | Catch Chance | Energy/sec | Personality |
|--------|-------|--------------|------------|-------------|
| Common | Gray | 80% | 1 | Friendly |
| Uncommon | Green | 60% | 2 | Playful |
| Rare | Blue | 40% | 5 | Mysterious |
| Legendary | Purple | 20% | 10 | Mischievous |
| Mythic | Gold | 5% | 25 | Chaotic |

### Bringing Ghosts Home
- **Mechanic**: Click HQ button to bring caught ghosts
- **Transport**: Animation shows ghost floating home
- **Storage**: Ghost stored in HQ (limited by room upgrades)
- **Energy Production**: Ghosts start generating Ecto-Energy immediately
- **Passive Income**: No action needed (always running)

### Energy Production
- **Base Generation**: 1 ghost = 1 energy/second (varies by rarity)
- **Training Boost**: Train ghosts to increase production rate
- **Room Multipliers**: HQ room upgrades multiply all production
- **Total Formula**: `(ghost_energy × ghost_count × room_multiplier)`

**Example**:
- 5 Common ghosts: 5 energy/sec
- Upgrade to "Ghost Chamber v2": ×1.5 multiplier → 7.5 energy/sec

### Ghost Training
- **What it does**: Improve ghost stats
- **Requirements**: Ecto-Energy
- **Stats**:
  - **Catch Speed**: Faster to catch in field
  - **Energy Output**: Produces more energy
  - **Happiness**: Unlocks special abilities
- **Time**: Training takes real-time (can be sped up with boosts)
- **Max Level**: Level 10 per ghost

**Training Costs**:
- Level 1→2: 50 energy
- Level 2→3: 100 energy
- Level 3→4: 200 energy
- And so on... (exponential)

### HQ Tycoon System

Players manage an HQ base with upgradable rooms.

#### Available Rooms

1. **Ghost Chamber** (Starting)
   - Stores ghosts
   - Upgrade: Doubles storage space each time
   - Level 1: 5 slots
   - Level 2: 10 slots
   - Level 3: 20 slots
   - Upgrades energy production by ×1.2 per level

2. **Training Facility**
   - Trains ghosts faster
   - Upgrade: Reduces training time
   - Level 1: Normal speed
   - Level 2: ×1.5 speed
   - Level 3: ×2 speed
   - Cost increases per upgrade

3. **Energy Reactor**
   - Boosts all energy production
   - Upgrade: Increases multiplier
   - Level 1: ×1.0 multiplier (no boost)
   - Level 2: ×1.5 multiplier
   - Level 3: ×2.0 multiplier
   - Very expensive

4. **Research Lab** (Unlock later)
   - Unlock new ghost types
   - Unlock special abilities
   - Unlock new zones

5. **Boss Arena** (Unlock later)
   - Fight boss ghosts
   - Earn special rewards
   - Permanent stat bonuses

### Zone Progression

#### Available Zones

**Forest** (Unlocked)
- Easy difficulty
- Common/Uncommon ghosts
- Good for early game
- Relaxing vibes

**Graveyard** (Cost: 500 energy)
- Medium difficulty
- Uncommon/Rare ghosts
- More efficient energy gain
- Spooky atmosphere

**Haunted Mansion** (Cost: 5,000 energy)
- Hard difficulty
- Rare/Legendary ghosts
- Best energy efficiency
- Creepy vibes

**Dark Dimension** (Cost: 50,000 energy)
- Very hard difficulty
- Legendary/Mythic ghosts
- Extreme efficiency
- Chaotic visuals

**Boss Zone** (Cost: 100,000 energy + defeat 5 bosses)
- Legendary boss ghosts only
- One-time battles
- Massive rewards
- Unlocked after progress

### Boss Ghosts

- **What**: Special ghost encounters with high HP and attacks
- **Where**: Available in zones after reaching certain levels
- **Reward**: Exclusive ghost + large energy bonus
- **Defeat**: Use trained ghosts to fight
- **Battle Mechanic**: Simple turn-based (for now)

### Auto Systems (Gamepass Features)

#### Auto-Catch
- Automatically catches ghosts at set intervals
- Cost: 699 Robux (one-time)
- Benefit: Passive catching without clicking
- Can be toggled on/off

#### Auto-Train
- Automatically trains ghosts
- Cost: 499 Robux (one-time)
- Benefit: Passive training progression
- Prioritizes lower-level ghosts

#### Double Energy
- Multiplies all energy production by ×2
- Cost: 399 Robux (one-time)
- Benefit: 2× faster progression
- Stacks with other multipliers

### Monetization

#### GamePasses (One-time purchases)
- **Auto-Catch**: 699 Robux (automatic catching)
- **Auto-Train**: 499 Robux (automatic training)
- **Double Energy**: 399 Robux (×2 production)
- **VIP Zone**: 799 Robux (exclusive zone)
- **Extra Storage**: 299 Robux (×2 ghost slots)

#### Developer Products (Consumables)
- **Energy Pack**: 100 Robux = 1,000 energy
- **Ghost Egg**: 299 Robux = Random ghost (1-5 rarity)
- **Boss Ticket**: 199 Robux = Boss fight ticket
- **Training Boost**: 99 Robux = ×2 training speed for 1 hour

## Progression Timeline

### Early Game (First Hour)
- Unlock Forest zone
- Catch common ghosts
- Train first ghost
- Upgrade Ghost Chamber once
- Understand core loop

### Mid Game (1-5 Hours)
- Unlock Graveyard zone
- Build ghost collection (5-10 ghosts)
- Upgrade multiple rooms
- Earn 10,000+ energy
- Start fighting bosses

### Late Game (5+ Hours)
- Unlock Mansion zone
- Have 15-25 ghosts
- Multiple room upgrades
- Earning 100+ energy/sec
- Accessible to all zones

### End Game
- Unlock Dark Dimension
- Complete all rooms
- Fight all bosses
- Catch Mythic ghosts
- Achievement hunting

## Balance Notes

- Early progression should feel rewarding
- Mid-game: New zones every 30-60 minutes
- Late-game: Long-term goals (cosmetics, achievements)
- GamePasses should feel optional, not required
- Energy inflation is intentional (exponential growth)

## FAQ

**Q: Can I lose ghosts?**
A: No, your ghosts are permanent once caught.

**Q: What if I run out of storage?**
A: You can't catch more until you upgrade Ghost Chamber.

**Q: Can I trade ghosts with friends?**
A: Not in this version (planned for future).

**Q: How long does training take?**
A: Depends on level. Level 1→2 takes ~5 minutes. Scales exponentially.

**Q: Is there a "reset" button?**
A: Not yet. This is permanent progression.
