## Design Philosophy

### Why These Colors?

**Purple & Blue (Background):**
- Spooky, mystical theme (ghosts, haunted)
- Premium feel (unlike bright primary colors)
- Roblox trending palette (similar to hit tycoons)
- Good contrast with light text

**Cyan, Magenta, Yellow (Highlights):**
- High saturation = visible on mobile
- Neon aesthetic = modern gaming vibe
- Complimentary colors = visually pleasing
- Energetic = conveys action/progression

### Why The Ghost Design?

**Features:**
- **Big eyes:** Makes it cute/approachable (not scary)
- **Happy mouth:** Positive emotion, fun vibe
- **Wavy bottom:** Classic ghost silhouette, instantly recognizable
- **Glowing effect:** Shows power/energy mechanics in game

### Why Multiple Ghosts (Thumbnail)?

**Represents:**
- Collection element (players catch ghosts)
- Variety (120+ ghost types)
- Teamwork/companions (ghosts work together)

## File Formats

### Assets to Create

1. **thumbnail.png** (1024×1024px)
   - Source: `assets/thumbnail.html`
   - Format: PNG (supports transparency)
   - Size: ~100-300KB (after compression)
   - Purpose: Game listing thumbnail on Roblox homepage

2. **icon.png** (512×512px)
   - Source: `assets/icon.html`
   - Format: PNG (supports transparency)
   - Size: ~50-150KB (after compression)
   - Purpose: Game icon (if Roblox supports it), also used in Discord/social
